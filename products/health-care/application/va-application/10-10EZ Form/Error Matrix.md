## All Errors - Full text
This is the full text mesasge and resolution for individual errors that can be encountered.
<details>
  
  <summary>System is undergoing maintenance</summary>
  
  **Message** <br>
  We're sorry.  The health care application is currently down while we fix a few things.  We'll be back up as soon as we can. <br>
  In the meantime, youc an call 877-222-8387, Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m. EST and press 2 to complete this application over the phone.
  
  **Resolution** <br>
  There are two options available:
  1. Wait until the application is back online and submit it then.
  2. Call customer support at 877-222-8387, Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m. EST.
  
  
  </details>
  
  <details>
  
  <summary>Authentication required to complete form</summary>
  
  **Message** <br>
  We're sorry for the interruption, but we've found some more information that we need you to review before you can apply for VA health care.  Please sign in to VA.gov to review.  If you don't have an account, you can create one now.
  
  **Resolution** <br>
  Follow the given instructions to log in to VA.gov and review the indicated information in order to continue.
  
  
  </details>
  
  <details>
  
  <summary>Form submission failed, progress is saved</summary>
  
  **Message** <br>

  **Resolution** <br>
  There are two options available:
  1. Continue to attempt to submit the application online.
  2. Call customer support at 877-222-8387, Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m. EST.
  
  
  </details>
